\chapter{Connected Sum}

\section{Connected Sum}

In this appendix, we recall the notion of connected sum, as required
principally in Section~1.4.

Given any two oriented manifolds \(\mathcal{A}\) and \(\mathcal{B}\) of the
same dimension, we first remove a small disc/ball in each.

We then paste them together, matching orientations, to get a new manifold
which we denote by \(\mathcal{A}\#\mathcal{B}\).
