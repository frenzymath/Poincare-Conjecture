\chapter*{About This Blueprint}

This project follows John M.~Lee, \emph{Introduction to Smooth Manifolds},
second edition, Graduate Texts in Mathematics 218 (Springer, 2013). A PDF
version can be downloaded at
\href{https://julianchaidez.net/materials/reu/lee_smooth_manifolds.pdf}{this link}.

Lee develops the language of smooth manifolds and smooth maps, tangent
vectors, immersions and embeddings, submanifolds, Sard's theorem, Lie groups,
and vector fields. These foundations support the Poincare project by fixing
the differential-topological structures and constructions used later in
three-manifold geometry and Ricci-flow arguments; this book is background for
those arguments, not a standalone proof of the Poincare conjecture.

The blueprint is a concise, independently worded distillation, not a verbatim
copy of the book, it only retains statements and proof ideas needed for
formalization. \textbf{We recommend you to read the blueprint alongside the book}. The \emph{dcref} tag records the corresponding theorem,
definition, or other source number in Lee's book and is the intended guide for
that comparison.

The present blueprint only covers Chapters 1--8 of the book (out of 22): smooth manifolds,
smooth maps, tangent vectors, submersions and embeddings, submanifolds, Sard's
theorem, Lie groups, and vector fields.
