\chapter*{About This Blueprint}

This project formalizes the Poincaré Conjecture, for this purpose we 
build this blueprint, reorganizing the mathematical ideas in a way that 
is fitting better our formalization purposes.

This blueprint is under construction, it is expected to evolve alongside with 
the progressing formalization. 

It relies heavily on external resources such as Morgan and Tian~\cite{MorganTian},
do Carmo~\cite{doCarmo}, and others~\cite{KleinerLott,CaoZhu,Topping,ChowKnopf,ChowEtAl,Petersen,LeeRiemannian,LeeSmooth,CheegerGromovTaylor,Hatcher,Evans,GilbargTrudinger,HanLin}.

\chapter{The Poincar\'e Conjecture}

\begin{theorem}[Poincar\'e Conjecture]
\label{thm:poincare-conjecture}
A closed, smooth, simply connected $3$-manifold is diffeomorphic to $S^3$.
\end{theorem}
